ABOUT

In 2016, Our Founder – Samantha, during an international trip visited a Burger restaurant which had multiple burger options & bold flavours like PB&J, classic cheese burger which were native to the country. That’s when the idea clicked – why can’t India has its own burger chain which sells varieties of burgers with unique and varied flavours that every Indian can relate to. That’s when The Burger and Co. was born!
